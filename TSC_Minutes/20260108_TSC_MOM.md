# Specification of SEAPATH
A Draft will be started next week.
Stefan Richter (RedHat) leads the work.
This specification are needed to propose a commercial offer of SEAPATH for LTS support. It will be discussed the choice of some SEAPATH components and their replacement.
A reminder has been done that this work will not make SEAPATH a RedHat product.
This specification are needed to add diversity in the SEAPATH support (Yocto/Debian/CentOS, etc).
It will be shared to the community and opensourced.
